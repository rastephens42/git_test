Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • Beef stock or Ingredients to make beef stock
  • Potatoes
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Flank steak
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  1. Bring beef stock to a boil
  2. Add hardy vegetables, like potatoes
  3. Add spices, as much as needed for taste
  4. After bringing to a boil, add your flank steak
  5. Leave on a rolling boil for at least two hours to add flavor
  6. Let cool and serve
